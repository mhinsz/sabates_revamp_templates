Eye Doctors in Kansas City

Our Doctors. Your eye care.

You deserve the very best eye care. And at Sabates Eye Centers, we’re committed to delivering it. That’s why all of our physicians are fellowship-trained doctors, researchers specializing in multiple areas of eye care, and professors at the University of Missouri-Kansas City (UMKC) School of Medicine, training the next generation of ophthalmologists. So you know you’re getting the highest level of eye care from physicians on the cutting edge of advances in treatments, detection, prevention and surgical techniques. Plus, all of our ophthalmologists and optometrists practice at multiple Sabates Eye Centers locations. So the eye care you need is always just minutes away.
